About Daylight Resort

Welcome to Daylight Resort, your perfect getaway for both daycation and staycation experiences. Nestled in a picturesque location, our resort offers a blend of luxury, comfort, and natural beauty. Whether you're looking for a day of relaxation or an extended stay, we have the perfect package for you.

Daycation Packages

  • Access to resort facilities for a full day
  • Use of swimming pools, fitness center, and spa
  • Complimentary non-alcoholic welcome drink
  • Gourmet lunch included in select packages
  • Optional add-ons like spa treatments or recreational activities

Staycation Packages

  • Variety of room types to choose from, including deluxe rooms and suites
  • All-inclusive meals at our gourmet restaurants
  • Access to all resort amenities throughout your stay
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi and parking
  • Special activities and entertainment programs

Important Information

  • Bookings must be made at least one day in advance
  • No alcohol is served or allowed on the premises
  • Outside food is not permitted; all meals are included in the packages
  • Flexible check-in and check-out times available (subject to availability)
