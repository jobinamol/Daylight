Your Daylight Experience

Daycation Guide

  1. Choose your package online
  2. Select your preferred date
  3. Arrive and check-in (9 AM - 11 AM)
  4. Enjoy resort facilities until 6 PM
  5. Pre-book spa treatments & activities

Staycation Guide

  1. Select package and dates online
  2. Complete booking process
  3. Check-in at 2 PM (early check-in available)
  4. Enjoy full resort access during your stay
  5. Check-out by 12 PM (late check-out option)

Important Notes

  • Advance reservations recommended
  • Bring valid ID for check-in
  • Outside food not permitted
  • Inform us of special requests in advance
  • Check cancellation policy when booking
